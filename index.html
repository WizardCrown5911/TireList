Forge TierList

Free Online Tier List Maker

Forge TierList is a free tier list builder for ranking anything you care about. Create S-to-D ranking boards, add items using text cards or searched images, reorder by dragging, save your lists to a personal dashboard, and export finished tier lists as PNG images — all in your browser.

A tier list is a ranking system that sorts items into labelled tiers — typically S, A, B, C, and D — from best to worst or most to least preferred. Tier lists are popular for ranking video game characters, films, albums, foods, sports players, and anything else where opinions differ. Making one is a fast way to crystallise your preferences and share them visually.

Key features

  • Drag-and-drop cards between S, A, B, C, and D tiers
  • Auto-generate text artwork for each item name
  • Search public images via Wikimedia Commons and Openverse
  • Upload your own images directly onto any card
  • Save multiple lists to a cloud dashboard with Google sign-in
  • Export the finished board as a high-resolution PNG file
  • Add custom tiers, rename labels, and pick tier colours
  • Import and export tier lists as JSON for backup or sharing

How it works

Type a list title such as "Best Nintendo Switch Games" or "Favourite Pizza Toppings" and click Find from title to automatically generate related items. You can also type your own items manually, one per line. Each new item gets an auto-generated text card showing its name. Once items are in the pool, drag them into whichever tier fits. When you are happy with the ranking, export it as a PNG or save it to your dashboard.

No account is needed to build and export a tier list. Sign in with Google to unlock cloud saving so your lists are safe across devices. Signed-in users can also mark favourites and sort their saved lists by last updated, title, or item count.
