About Forge TierList

Forge TierList is a free, browser-based tier list maker that lets you rank anything using drag-and-drop cards, automatic image search, and cloud saving. No installation required — open the app, type a topic, and start ranking.

What it does

The app gives you a ranking board with S, A, B, C, and D tiers by default. You add items to a pool, drag them into the tier that fits, and export the finished board as a PNG image. Every item gets an auto-generated text card so you can start ranking immediately without hunting for images. When you need a real picture, the image search finds public domain and Creative Commons images from Wikimedia Commons and Openverse.

Key capabilities

  • Free to use with no account required for building and exporting
  • Drag-and-drop interface for reordering items and moving them between tiers
  • Auto-generated text artwork for every item name, customisable with colour
  • Image search powered by Wikimedia Commons and Openverse
  • Upload your own images from any device
  • Google sign-in for saving multiple lists to a personal dashboard
  • Export to PNG for sharing or export to JSON for backup
  • Customisable tier labels, colours, and preset themes
  • AI-assisted item suggestions based on your list title

Privacy

Forge TierList does not collect personal data from anonymous visitors. When you sign in with Google, your saved tier lists are stored in a private Firebase database linked to your Google account. Lists are not shared publicly and are only accessible by the signed-in user. Anonymous usage data may be collected by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads.
